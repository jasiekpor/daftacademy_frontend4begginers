ABOUT

STRAIGHT FORM LUBLIN

6BM is a young band which has it's origins in the city of Lublin, playing music, described as electrorock.

Band members aim to convey emotions, reflexion but also hope.

They are ispired by varoius genres, like: alternative rock, nu-metal, synthwave or even drum'n'bass.

As for now they reased ther debut EP - TRINITY and they started working on their first full length album.
